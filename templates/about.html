About Neohuman Visual Search

Neohuman Visual Search is a search engine leveraging self-supervised deep learning to provide efficient similarity search for entities within images.
This is currently only a small demo with around 5M images from Flickr and a deep learning model that will still need some fine tuning. Given that the image database consists of social media posts, the retrieval works best with pictures of humans, pets etc.
The lowest hanging fruit in terms of improving the performace are 1) to increase the image database size by at least a couple of orders of magnitude and 2) fine tune/ re-train the model (don't quite have the time or resources for that ATM).
